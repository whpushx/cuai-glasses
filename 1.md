Product Requirement Document (PRD): cuai.ai - Enterprise AI Wearable Solutions
1. Project Overview
Project Name: cuai.ai

Brand Concept: * "See You" (CU): Emphasizing the visual and optical nature of AI glasses and "seeing" the future of work.

"Custom" (CU): Focusing on bespoke B2B solutions, tailoring hardware to specific corporate needs.

Target Audience: B2B enterprises, HR/IT procurement departments, and premium corporate gift buyers.

Technical Requirement: High-end static website (HTML5, Tailwind CSS, Vanilla JavaScript). No Database Required.

2. Visual & Brand Identity
Design Philosophy: Future-tech, sleek, and highly professional. The UI should feel like a premium hardware brand (e.g., Apple or Zeiss).

Color Palette:

Primary: Midnight Navy (#0F172A) for professionalism.

Accent: Crystal Blue (#38BDF8) representing lenses/clarity.

Secondary: Soft Slate (#64748B) for text and secondary elements.

UI Style: Glassmorphism (semi-transparent backgrounds), smooth scroll animations, and high-quality product photography placeholders.

3. Website Structure
3.1 Global Navigation
Left: cuai.ai (Bold, clean typography).

Center: Solutions | Services | Use Cases | About.

Right: [Request a Quote] (Call-to-Action button with a hover glow effect).

3.2 Hero Section (The Hook)
Headline: "See the Future. Custom for Your Business."

Sub-headline: The world’s first B2B platform dedicated to AI wearable integration—from professional prescription lens fitting to bespoke corporate branding.

Visual: A high-resolution hero image of AI glasses with a focus on lens clarity and premium finish.

CTAs: [Explore Solutions] [View B2B Services]

3.3 Core Services (3-Column Card Layout)
Service A: Precision Rx Lens Fitting

Description: Professional optical integration for all major AI glasses. We provide custom-ground prescription lenses (Near-sighted/Astigmatism) optimized for AR displays.

Service B: Corporate Branding & Customization

Description: High-precision laser engraving, custom frame colors, and enterprise-logo packaging for AI glasses and smart rings.

Service C: Enterprise Fleet Maintenance

Description: Full-cycle support including bulk cleaning, sensor calibration, and part replacement to keep your team’s wearables in peak condition.

3.4 Industry Use Cases (Vertical Scroll or Grid)
Logistics & Warehousing: Anti-glare HUD lenses for hands-free scanning.

Executive Gifting: Custom-engraved AI rings and glasses sets for VIP events.

Technical Support: Ruggedized accessory kits for engineers using remote-assist AI eyewear.

3.5 The "CU Cost Estimator" (Interactive Static Component)
Function: A simple client-side JS calculator.

Inputs: [Select Device Model] + [Choose Service: Lens/Branding/Accessories] + [Input Quantity].

Output: Display a "Volume Discount Grade" (e.g., Bronze, Silver, Gold) and an estimated turnaround time.

Action: [Download Inquiry PDF] (Simulated button).

3.6 Contact & Footer
Contact Form: Name, Company, Fleet Size, and Message.

Direct Contact: WhatsApp, WeChat QR Code, and info@cuai.ai.

Footer Note: "Cu (See You) for Vision. Cu (Custom) for Excellence. cuai.ai — Seeing the Future Clearly."

4. Technical Specs & SEO
Responsiveness: Mobile-first design; must look perfect on iPhone 15/16 and desktop monitors.

Performance: Fast-loading static assets with optimized SVG icons.

SEO Metadata:

Title: cuai.ai | Custom AI Glasses & Smart Ring B2B Solutions

Description: Enterprise-grade accessories and services for AI wearables. We offer prescription lens fitting, laser engraving, and fleet management for AI glasses.
